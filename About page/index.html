About Manchester Textiles

Discover our legacy of quality and innovation in the textile industry.

Our History

Founded in 1920, Manchester Textiles has been at the forefront of textile manufacturing for over a century. What started as a small family business has grown into a global leader, known for our commitment to quality, sustainability, and innovation.

Throughout the years, we've adapted to changing technologies and market demands while maintaining our core values of craftsmanship and customer satisfaction. From traditional weaving techniques to modern sustainable practices, we've always strived to produce the finest fabrics.

Our Mission & Values

Quality First

We are dedicated to producing textiles of the highest quality, using only the finest materials and most advanced techniques.

Sustainability

Environmental responsibility is at the heart of our operations. We strive to minimize our impact on the planet through sustainable practices.

Innovation

We continuously invest in research and development to bring cutting-edge textile solutions to our customers.

Customer Focus

Our customers are our priority. We work closely with them to understand their needs and exceed their expectations.
